Interview Process Overview

Based on recent fresher interview experiences at IBM India for SRMIST students

1

Online Assessment

60-90 minutes

  • Aptitude: Number series, ratios, percentages, work & time
  • English: Vocabulary, idioms, sentence correction
  • Coding: 1-2 DSA problems (medium difficulty)
  • Programming MCQs: OOP, data structures, algorithms
Pro Tip: Practice on PrepInsta and LeetCode before the test
2

Technical Interview

45-60 minutes

  • DSA Questions: Arrays, strings, trees, stacks/queues
  • Language Fundamentals: OOP, memory management, pointers
  • Project Deep-Dive: Your final year project/internship
  • CS Theory: OS, DBMS, networking basics
Pro Tip: Be crystal clear about your project - what you did, challenges faced, and solutions
3

HR / Behavioural Round

20-30 minutes

  • About You: Tell me about yourself, strengths/weaknesses
  • Motivation: Why IBM? Why this role?
  • Career Goals: Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
  • Logistics: Relocation willingness, start date
Pro Tip: Research IBM's culture, recent projects, and have questions ready for the interviewer
